Privacy Policy Last updated: January 15, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Trusted Self Storage Professionals (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our internal accounting automation application (the “Application”). 1. Scope of This Policy This Application is an internal-use tool designed to automate accounting workflows for authorized users within Trusted Self Storage Professionals. It is not offered to the general public. 2. Information We Collect When you connect the Application to QuickBooks Online, we may access and process the following information: Company identification data (Company ID, company name) Accounting data such as: Journal entries Deposits Invoices Customers and vendors OAuth authentication tokens required to access the QuickBooks Online API We do not collect personal consumer data outside of what is necessary to perform internal accounting functions. 3. How We Use Information We use accessed information solely to: Automate accounting data entry Synchronize financial records between internal systems Generate accounting reports and journal entries Maintain audit trails for internal accounting purposes 4. Data Storage and Security OAuth tokens and accounting data are stored securely with access restricted to authorized personnel. Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are used to protect data from unauthorized access. Data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. 5. Data Retention Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill accounting, operational, and compliance requirements or as required by applicable law. 6. Third-Party Services The Application integrates with QuickBooks Online via official APIs. Use of QuickBooks Online is governed by Intuit’s applicable privacy policies and terms. 7. User Access Access to the Application is limited to authorized internal users. No consumer accounts or public user registration is supported. 8.
